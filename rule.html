Server Rules & Nation Guidelines

Everything you need to know to get started and stay within the community's rules.

Play Fair - No Cheating or Exploits

  • No hacking, cheating, X-ray, duping, or AI bots for mining.

Combat Rules

  • No Elytra or Riptide Tridents in combat.
  • No End Crystals, Respawn Anchors, or Bed Bombing in combat or PvP.
  • Do not enter spawn while in combat.
  • No farming kills on friends or other players.

Spawn Rules

  • No killing at spawn.
  • Do not destroy or build anything ugly at spawn.

Enchantment & Item Limits

  • Protection 3 (III) max.
  • Sharpness 4 (IV) max.
  • Power 3 (III) max.
  • Ender Pearls cooldown: 7 seconds.
  • No Netherite armor or tools (except pickaxe, hoe, and shovel).
  • Only Tier 1 potions allowed.
  • Thorns enchantment fully banned.
  • The mace is uncraftable and unobtainable.

Mods & Allowed Features

  • Banned: Freecam, Replay Mod (for locating players), Fast Place, Mini Map, Health Indicator mods
  • Allowed: Waypoints and World Map from mods

World Border

  • World border is 4.2k by 4.2k.

Civilization Rules

  • You need 6 to 8 members to start a civilization.
  • If your group has fewer than 6 members or any member is inactive for 7 days or more, your civilization will be disbanded.
  • There are 2 pre-built civilizations with ready structures.
  • Pre-built civilizations do not have to pay any maintenance fees.
  • Custom civilizations can be built anywhere with no pre-built structures.
  • Custom civilizations must pay a daily maintenance fee to stay active.

Bandit Rules

  • Bandits are lone players or small groups not affiliated with any nation.
  • Bandits can sabotage and wage wars but will face retaliation if caught.
  • Bandits can still earn achievements, titles, and participate in arena tournaments.
