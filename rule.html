Server Rules & Nation Guidelines

Everything you need to know to get started and stay within the community's rules.

Server Rules

  • Respect all players and staff members at all times.
  • No offensive language, harassment, or discrimination.
  • Cheating or exploiting bugs will result in a permanent ban.
  • Griefing, stealing, or destroying other players' property is prohibited.

Nation Rules

  • Each nation must pass its rules through the parliament before being declared.
  • All nations need a written constitution, defining their rules and responsibilities.
  • At least 6-8 players are required to form a nation.
  • A nation must have the essential structures: town hall, courtroom, etc.
  • Members must agree to all rules before the nation is declared.

Bandit Rules

  • Bandits are lone players or small groups not affiliated with any nation.
  • Bandits can sabotage and wage wars but will face retaliation if caught.
  • Bandits can still earn achievements, titles, and participate in arena tournaments.
